About Go!Runna

According to a PwC report in 2016, 47% of the 18 to 34 age group had changed their eating habits towards a healthier diet. In response to this, we have designed the world’s first energy boosting mint, specially for students in the UK.

Introducing Go!Runna, our healthy Guarana based mint, with convenient, affordable, healthy, sustainable and organic benefits.

The ‘Go!Runna Man’, as you can see here, is one of the key symbols of our brand, and is inspired by the Amazonian Guarana berry, the key ingredient in our product

So what’s in a Go Runna that makes it so different?

  • Go Runna is completely organic, because we don’t use any artificial ingredients.
  • Go Runna utilizes a naturally occurring source of caffeine, which is the Amazonian Guarana berry.
  • We also do not use artificial sweeteners, which contain potentially harmful ingredients such as aspartame. Instead, we use coconut sugar, a healthier alternative that contains calcium and iron.
  • Our natural binding ingredient is Acacia Gum, which has also been proven to be beneficial for lowering cholesterol.
  • Finally, peppermint extract provides the refreshing mint flavor, while also improving digestion.

    We believe Go Runna is positioned for success as a new product.

    It has a high relative customer benefit as it is a healthier and more affordable alternative to existing energy boosters in the market. We believe we are ideally positioned to fully take advantage of the current socio-cultural environment.

    In addition, it is compatible with the busy, on-the-go lifestyles of students, who already rely heavily on caffeine to get their work done.

    The benefits of Go Runna are easy to understand and communicate to our target audience, and they face a relatively low risk when deciding to try the product due to its price and convenience.

    Go Runna is also the only all-natural product in a saturated market.

    Thirdly, our product is sustainable and environmentally friendly. This means that our product is introducing incremental innovation to an otherwise generic market.
